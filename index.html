Clever-Thoughts Consulting

Providing consulting services for the design, fabrication, test and measurement procedures, and integration of linear and nonlinear optic systems and components. Special emphasis on guided-wave nonlinear-optical devices based on periodically poled ferroelectric materials, such as lithium niobate, lithium tantalate, as well as their doped and stoichiometric variants. Experienced with applications ranging from high-power visible light generation for projection, to low-noise, ultra-efficient traveling-wave frequency conversion for quantum optics.
